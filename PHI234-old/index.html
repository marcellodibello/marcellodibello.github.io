Philosophy of Law/Jurisprudence syllabus

What makes a written text a piece of law? Are laws human conventions? How should judges decide difficult cases? Why should we obey the law? Should we obey immoral laws? What is a crime? When can we hold people legally responsible? Why are people punished? What is proof beyond a reasonable doubt?
